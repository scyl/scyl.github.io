dis:Connect

A short animation made for the final final project in the Digital Media degree at UNSW. It's about the interaction between a human and an AI, and how we human can be so cold-hearted.

This project was done by Srorvimoul Nhim, Kriska Budiman and myself. I focused on the set modelling, rigging, texturing, vfx and rendering.
